My biggest fear isn’t that none of what I experience is real — instead being the opposite — that everything is real. That everything around me, be it the people I talk to, maybe even trust, are genuine; just not how I believe them to be. Almost a fear for them being real enough to judge me, to talk about me when I’m not around, to laugh collectively at how naive I am for believing I ever belonged. Glancing over at each other when I talk, with jokes about me behind my back, overanalysing my every word, movement, etc. Every awkward moment I stop for, getting only more entertainment, unknowingly the core of an awful joke. Every kind gesture secretly being mockery I don’t realise, me being the only one unaware.
