I don’t feel love. I don’t feel many emotions, it isn’t limited to love—yet love is the emotion that beams light through the cracks of my facade. For other emotions, I can easily fraud them with little to no problems. Nobody cares if I’m forcing myself to seem as if I’m present, calm, void of worry. Nobody cares if I lie about events being better than they were to make my existence seem less hellish. Yet, when it comes to love, dishonesty in emotion is never enough for anyone and I don’t blame them for that. So what is love to you? I see love as an emotional attachment that transcends beyond normality in platonic relationships, which are built on general care and respect. I feel platonic love, be it rarely, I do have few friends I truly care for. When it comes to romantic love, you also include emotional attachment, attraction & selflessness. The deep emotional pull others describe when in love is something I don’t relate to, I don’t believe I’ve ever felt it, and I’m not one to lie on something that people hold so dear to themselves. If I’m to say I love, that love isn’t a lone flower of the bunch, rather an entire bouquet. It’s not the fault of anyone else, how they act seems to have no correlation to how I feel when it comes to love, I truly believe my brains just wired in a strange way I can’t fix. That warm feeling people describe? I can’t fathom it; incomparable to a scale where i only feel the cold, rather the absence of a scale in the first place. I can’t imagine warmth in the boundaries of nothingness, like a blind man will never understand the beauty of nature. I don’t choose not to love, there’s nothing I crave like my desire to feel love. There is no decision made, the feeling just doesn’t exist within. I can act with kindness & responsibility whilst feeling no underlying chemistry. I don’t know why I’m like this, and I hope one day I do feel love as well as other emotions I lack, as living without them has shown to be difficult. Could it be because of love being made out to be a joke to me when I was young? Something else? No point dwelling on it since I can’t change the past, move forward. Part of me fears love because, although I don’t feel love, when I’m told I’m loved and they turn out to be lying, it leaves me thinking I might not be so alone in this absence, where love is the most interesting form of self-gaslighting since the dawn of time. When I’m told I’m cared for I have doubts, I don’t trust what people say easily, especially when I don’t understand why. Every time I was told I was loved turned out to be a lie, so I now sit here hesitant to accept what I’m told when it comes to how people feel about me. I don’t intend to hurt anyone when I say I don’t feel towards them in that sense, I’d just like to avoid misleading them into believing I can give something that I can not. I can respect you, be attracted to you, I’m still human, just without a couple important modules. I’m open about my uncertainty of any of this is real, so none of this should come as much of a surprise, I feel like a man with three senses in a world where everyone else has five, blind & deaf. You’re still so pretty I wish I had love to give but if I try I feel like a computer spitting out error messages. I don’t know what I’m doing, I need the right wiring. I’m sorry if I can’t give what you want but I really wish I could whenever I look into your eyes <3
