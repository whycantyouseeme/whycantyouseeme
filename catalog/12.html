Humanity has been the greatest plague to the material world. Human civilisation is a virus—expanding rapidly, disregarding what it replaces, as long as the end goal is met—to replace. We are flawed, any beauty we create is inevitably destroyed, the innate desire for something greater, we will sabotage others just to promote our own agenda. No other animal sees us as a net-positive to the world, yet still, we thrive off of the perfection nature has presented. We were never needed in the first place; in fact, the world would’ve been better off without us. We are Gods greatest mistake, it is no wonder she does not love us. Dona nobis pacem.
